Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Soft, delicious chocolate chip cookies are an amazing treat. They're a great snack, or if you're a little crazy, can even be an entire meal! (Note: I don't take responsibility for any damages to your health that may occur)

This recipe will show you how to bake cookies from scratch. If you're up to it, let's hop in!

Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons of salted butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips
